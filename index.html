What is the process like?

we will start by taking before & after photos. then, your artist will pre-draw the shape and then once the shape is agreed upon, your artist will get started. the color is chosen to match your natural color as close as possible with consideration of your skins complexion. once we have completed the procedure, after pictures/videos will be taken and the after care will be reviewed

do i need to do anything to my brows before the procedure?

the more grown out the better! once you have booked your appt, do not touch your tweezers/razorz/wax/thread, etc

what is ombre brows?

the ombre powder technique procedure gives a light to dark ombre enhancement to the eyebrows. it starts lighter at the front of the brow and gets darker at the tail. powder ombre gives a soft powder "makeup look" using a machine which deposits pigment into the skin. the pigment is deposited behind the brow hairs and can be customized to more dramatic or natural looking. it is amazing because it is suitable for all skin types compared to microblading.
